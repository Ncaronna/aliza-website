About Me

Art You'll Wear Forever.

I'm Aliza Nadine — a tattoo artist based in Phoenix, AZ with a passion for bold realism, fine line work, and custom script. Every tattoo I do is a one-of-a-kind piece of art built around your story.

I work out of Ink. Body Art in central Phoenix — a private, comfortable studio where you can relax and trust the process. Whether it's your first tattoo or your fiftieth, I'll make sure you leave with something you're proud to wear for life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the tattoo studio located?

Aliza works out of Ink. Body Art, located at 3508 N. 7th St., #125, Phoenix, AZ 85014. Free parking is available on-site. Get directions →

What tattoo styles does Aliza specialize in?

Aliza specializes in realism, fine-line, script, and flash tattoos. Whether you're looking for a photorealistic portrait, delicate botanical linework, elegant lettering, or a ready-to-go flash design, she brings each piece to life with precision and artistry.

How do I book a tattoo appointment?

Fill out the booking form on this page with your idea, preferred size, and placement. Aliza will follow up to discuss your design, provide a quote, and schedule your session. A 30% deposit is required to book — it goes toward your total, and the remaining balance is paid at the end of your appointment.

How much does a tattoo cost?

Day rates are $850 for a full day and $650 for a half day. Smaller custom pieces are quoted individually. On weekends (Friday–Sunday, 10 PM–4 AM) Aliza runs late-night flash specials: $60 (1"), $80 (2"), and $100 (3"). See full rates →

Can Aliza cover up or rework an old tattoo?

Yes! Aliza has extensive experience with cover-ups and reworks. Send a clear photo of your existing tattoo along with your ideas through the booking form and she'll let you know what's possible.
