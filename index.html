About Me

Art You'll Wear Forever.

I'm Aliza Nadine — a Phoenix-based tattoo artist with a passion for bold realism, fine line work, and custom script. I believe every tattoo should be a piece of art that tells your story.

Based at Jacob J Ink in Phoenix, my studio is a private, comfortable space where you can relax and trust the process. Whether it's your first tattoo or your twentieth, I take the time to make sure you leave with something you're proud to wear for life.
